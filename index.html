About Us - New Era Logistics

Founded by Wiseman in September 2023, New Era Logistics has quickly become one of the most respected virtual trucking companies in Euro Truck Simulator 2 (ETS2) and American Truck Simulator (ATS).

Our mission is to provide a friendly, professional, and supportive environment where drivers can grow, succeed, and have fun. We offer a variety of solo and group missions, exciting events, and a global community of drivers from around the world.

What started as a small group of passionate truckers has grown into a top-ranking VTC, holding the Number 1 spot for over six months. Our commitment to providing a great experience for all drivers is what makes us stand out. We believe in teamwork, respect, and constantly striving for excellence, while also making sure every member feels welcome and valued.

Our Values - New Era Logistics

Professionalism: We maintain a high standard of professionalism in everything we do, from driving to communication.

Teamwork: We believe that working together as a team is key to our success. Everyone plays an important role.

Fun: We love trucking and aim to keep it enjoyable for all members with regular events and social activities.

Support: New Era Logistics is here to help every member succeed, offering guidance and mentorship to drivers of all skill levels.

Our Vision

As we look to the future, New Era Logistics aims to remain a leader in the virtual trucking community, setting new standards of excellence and enjoyment for years to come. We are always striving to improve and grow, ensuring that every driver feels like an important part of our family.
