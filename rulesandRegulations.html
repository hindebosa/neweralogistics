Rules and regulations - New Era Logistics

Article 1: Introduction

New Era Logistics VTC is a community-driven Virtual Trucking Company (VTC) that aims to provide a realistic and enjoyable Euro Truck Simulator experience. This Code of Conduct outlines the rules and expectations for all members.

Article 2: General Conduct

  1. Respect and Professionalism: Treat fellow members, other players, and TruckersMP staff with respect and professionalism.
  2. Language and Behavior: Refrain from using profanity, discriminatory language, or engaging in toxic behavior.
  3. Community Engagement: Participate in VTC events, convoys, and other activities to build a strong community.

Article 3: Game-Related Rules

  1. Convoy Mod Policy: Refrain from using Steam Workshop mods during official convoys.
  2. Company Skin:
    2.1. Always use the official New Era Logistics VTC skin when driving in TruckersMP servers.
  3. TruckersMP Rules:
    3.1. Adhere to all TruckersMP rules and regulations at all times.
  4. Community Engagement: Participate in VTC events, convoys, and other activities to build a strong community.
  5. Convoy Participation: Participate in at least: 2 external convoys per month (organized by other VTCs or communities). 1 internal convoy per month (organized by New Era Logistics VTC).

Article 4: Convoy Rules

  1. Convoy Etiquette: Follow standard convoy procedures, including:
    1.1. Maintaining a safe distance from the truck in front.
    1.2. Avoiding overtaking or cutting off fellow convoy members.
    1.3. Keeping the convoy channel clear for important announcements.
  2. Convoy Attendance: Attend convoys on time and remain for the duration.

Article 5: Tracking and Logging

  1. TrucksBook Integration: All members are required to connect their TrucksBook account to the VTC's TrucksBook company page.
  2. Tracking Requirements: Members must enable the following tracking stems in TrucksBook:
    2.1. Delivery Tracking: Track all deliveries made while representing the VTC.
    2.2. Distance Tracking: Track all distances driven while representing the VTC.
    2.3. Fuel Consumption Tracking: Track all fuel consumed while representing the VTC.

Article 6: Activity Requirements

  1. Minimum Distance Driven:
    1.1. Members must drive a minimum of 30,000 km per month while representing the VTC.
  2. Regular Activity:
    2.1. Maintain regular activity within the VTC, including participating in convoys and engaging with fellow members.

Article 7: Membership Expectations

  1. Communication: Respond to messages and requests from VTC staff and members in a timely manner.

Article 8: Disciplinary Action

  1. Warning: A formal warning issued by VTC staff.
  2. Probation: Temporary restrictions on convoy participation or other VTC activities.
  3. Membership Revocation: Permanent removal from the VTC.

Article 9: Leave Policy

  1. Request Submission: Submit leave requests through the portal or email HR@neweralogistics.co.za at least 48 hours in advance.

Approval Timeline:

  1. HR will confirm or deny requests within 24 hours.
  2. Extended Absences: Leave longer than two months is only permitted for mid-year or end-of-year exams. Members requiring longer breaks must resign but are welcome to reapply when ready to return.

Complaints and Conflict Resolution

  1. We handle concerns with fairness, aiming for prompt and respectful resolutions.

How to Submit: File complaints through the portal or email HR with relevant details and evidence (e.g., screenshots).

  1. Resolution Process:
  2. 1. HR acknowledges complaints within 24 hours.
  3. 2. Investigations are completed within 3 business days, involving discussions with all parties.
  4. 3. Resolutions are communicated within 5 business days.

Escalation: If unresolved, issues may be referred to the COO by HR for final review.

Confidentiality:

  1. All matters are handled discreetly to protect involved members

Article 10: Amendments and Updates

  1. This Code of Conduct may be amended or updated at any time by VTC staff. Members will be notified of any changes.

Conclusion

By joining New Era Logistics VTC, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to abide by this Code of Conduct.
